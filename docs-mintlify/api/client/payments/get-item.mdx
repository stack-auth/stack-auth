---
title: "Get Item"
description: "Retrieve details of a specific item such as credits or quotas associated with a customer account."
api: "GET /api/v1/payments/items/{customer_type}/{customer_id}/{item_id}"
---

## Request

### Path Parameters

<ParamField path="customer_type" type="string" required>
  The classification identifying the customer category.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="customer_id" type="string" required>
  The unique identifier for the customer.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="item_id" type="string" required>
  The unique identifier of the specific item being requested.
</ParamField>

## Response

<ResponseField name="200" type="object">
  Successful response with item details including credits and quota allocations.
</ResponseField>
