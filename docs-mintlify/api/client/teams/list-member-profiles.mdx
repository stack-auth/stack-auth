---
title: "List Team Member Profiles"
description: "List the profiles of team members. You must be a member of the team and have the $read_members permission to list all profiles."
api: "GET /api/v1/team-member-profiles"
---

You can always access your own profile by setting `user_id` to `me`.

## Request

### Query Parameters

<ParamField query="team_id" type="string" required>
  The identifier for the team you belong to on the client.
</ParamField>

<ParamField query="user_id" type="string">
  Filter results by user ID. Use `me` to retrieve your own profile.
</ParamField>

## Response

<ResponseField name="200" type="object">
  Successful response with the list of team member profiles.
</ResponseField>
